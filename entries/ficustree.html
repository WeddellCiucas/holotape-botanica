FICUS TREE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a pretty diverse genus of woody trees, shrubs and vines, but the ones I have found dotting the artificial landscape of New Vegas Strip is the Ficus Benjamina. 
⸙ Also known as the Weeping Fig, it prefers tropical and subtropical regions. 
⸙ The sap is latex-like and can trigger allergies in latex-sensitive individuals.
⸙ It's also possible to cause allergens and respiratory reactions to sensitive individuals if it's potted indoors.
⸙ Leaf extracts show potent actions against drug-resistant bacteria and aid wound closure.
⸙ Other leaf fractions can demonstrate pain relief, fever reduction and inflammatory control.
⸙ High dosages can be toxic.
