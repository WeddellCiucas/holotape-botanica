RAZORGRAIN
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ A golden-colored, grass-like grain.
⸙ It's widely cultivated across the wasteland.
⸙ It also resembles the old pre-War wheat grain. 
⸙ It can be consumed raw, but it doesn't provide much nutrition.
⸙ However, it can be ground into flour that can be used in baking goods, such as bread and pastries.
⸙ It can also be fermented into alcoholic beverages.
⸙ Some settlements also cook/fry the grains.
