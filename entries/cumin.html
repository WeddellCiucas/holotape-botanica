────────────────────────────
CUMIN
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a slender, branched herb with fine, feathery leaves  
⸙ The flowers are small, white or pink  
⸙ The seeds are elongated, ridged and brownish  
⸙ Thrives in hot and arid climates  
⸙ The seeds have digestive and antimicrobial properties  
⸙ Amazing for flavoring  
⸙ Dried spice bundle can be used to mask chem odor.
