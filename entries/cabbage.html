────────────────────────────
CABBAGE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a dense-head biennial vegetable. It thrives in temperate zones and prefers well-drained soils. There's a high variety of sub-species, each with their own particularities and climate resistances. 
⸙ It's rich in fibers and has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
⸙ Contains anthocyanins and sulfur compounds that combat inflammation and free radicals.
⸙ It's a nutritious food source for digestive health. 
⸙ Historically, leaves were applied during World War I for cooling treatment of trench foot.
