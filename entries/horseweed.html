HORSEWEED
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a hardy herb in the Aster family.
⸙ It can grow up pretty tall, reaching up to 5 ft. with hairy stems and narrow leaves.
⸙ Tiny white ray florets with yellow centers adorn the branched clusters.
⸙ It thrives on roadsides, fields and rubble.
⸙ It's notably drought-resistant.
⸙ Historically, leaves have been brewed to treat dysentery, sore throats, fevers and respiratory issues.
⸙ Contains antioxidants and diuretic compounds, used to manage menstrual disorders, hemorrhoids and other digestive issues.
⸙ Dried stems are reliable tinder for fire-starting.
