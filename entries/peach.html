────────────────────────────
PEACH
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a deciduous tree known for its short lifespan, though lately it has been thriving in the hydroponics labs of functional Vaults.
⸙ They normally grow in temperate zones. 
⸙ They are known for their beautiful spring blossoms and sweet, fuzzy fruits.
⸙ The fruits are composed of 88-90% water so they are highly hydrating, and pack a good amount of potassium. 
⸙ They also have minerals like magnesium and iron, and improve collagen production.
