────────────────────────────
CEDAR TREE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Tall evergreen tree in the pine family.  
⸙ Has horizontal branches and soft needles;  
⸙ The bark is gray and aromatic when cut.  
⸙ It grows in cool, dry highland climates, in rocky soil  
⸙ The resin sap is used for insect bites and waterproofing gear.  
⸙ Burning the bark is often used by locals in their purification rituals.  
⸙ The needles contain vitamin C, and they are useful in steam inhalations or brewed in tea to alleviate cold symptoms.  
⸙ Not to be mistaken with the Red Cedar or other juniper trees, that produce toxic berries.
