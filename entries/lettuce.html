LETTUCE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a leafy vegetable in the daisy family, known for its tender, edible leaves.
⸙ The leaves grow in compact rosettes of smooth or curly leaves, or in loose-leaf clusters.
⸙ They grow about 8-12 inches, and can range in color between green and red, depending on their species.
⸙ The flowers are small and yellow.
⸙ It prefers cool and temperate climates.
⸙ The leaves are high in water content, vitamins, fibers and iron, and are used in various dishes.
⸙ Apparently, it can have a mild sedative effect due to lactucarium or the "lettuce opium", used to promote calmness and sleep.
⸙ Can also be used as a digestive aid.
