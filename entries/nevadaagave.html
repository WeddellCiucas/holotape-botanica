NEVADA AGAVE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Has large, thick rosette of greenish-gray pointy leaves and spiny edges;
⸙ Grows in rocky, sun-exposed areas;
⸙ Heavily drought resistant; 
⸙  It flowers once in a lifetime and then it dies.
⸙ The sap can be used to apply on wounds and burns to slow down infection
⸙ Can be used as an anti-venom binder, to draw away toxins from wounds;
⸙ The pulp can be eaten for its mildly hydrating properties as the gel is water-rich;
⸙ Culinary enthusiasts can extract agave syrup and use as a sugar replacer.
