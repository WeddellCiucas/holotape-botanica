MAPLE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ They are either large trees or large shrubs recognized for their lobed leaves and bright autumn foliage.
⸙ The flowers are small yellow-green or red. They grow in clusters before the leaves appear in spring.
⸙ The fruits are winged samaras that spin when they fall.
⸙ The bark is smooth and gray on young trees, that grows furrows as it matures.
⸙ It prefers cool and temperate zones.
⸙ The maple sap contains small amounts of sugars, minerals and amino acids.
⸙ The bark and leaves contain anti-inflammatory properties, and the bark infusions are often used in digestive tonics and sore throat relief.
⸙ The wood is especially used for furniture, tools and musical instruments.
