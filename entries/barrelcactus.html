BARREL CACTUS
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Large, barrel-shaped cactus covered in dense ribs with heavy yellow, red or brown spines  
⸙ Produces bright yellow or orange flowers  
⸙ Fruits appear after blooming, also pineapple or barrel-shaped  
⸙ They thrive in arid, desert regions  
⸙ The fruit can be eaten raw or cooked and provides mild hydration  
⸙ The pulp can be boiled and crafted into a salve  
⸙ Can also be made into a sunscreen paste  
⸙ Overripe fruit can cause dizziness and vomiting
