ASH BLOSSOM
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a Commonwealth plant that grows almost exclusively in heavily irradiated bodies of water 
⸙ They can be picked from greyish-black trailing vines, low-ground similar to melons
⸙ Despite their radiation intake, they have healing properties
⸙ It can be brewed into a herbal antimicrobial
⸙ They also help add resistance to fatigue and lethargy effects
