────────────────────────────
BOXWOOD
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ They grow 2-10 feet tall as hedges.  
⸙ They are dense, evergreen shrubs, with small and round dark green leaves  
⸙ They grow slow, and prefer temperate to warm climates  
⸙ The leaves are toxic to some insects and animals, can be crafted into a makeshift insect repellent  
⸙ The wood is dense and smooth, ideal for wood carving
