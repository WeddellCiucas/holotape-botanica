GIANT PITCHER
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Carnivorous, ground-dwelling plant, easily distinguishable by the "pitcher" that traps and digests insects and small animals
⸙ Bright green or red-purple in color, with spotted or veined patterns
⸙ Leaves are wine-like
⸙ Lives in typically marshy or tropical conditions
⸙ The digestive fluid can be harvested to craft paralytic chems or sedatives 
⸙ The pitcher walls contain corrosive compounds 
⸙ The nectar, or the rim secretion, can be used for its pheromone properties
