────────────────────────────
GINGER
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a tall, reed-like plant with narrow green leaves, growing from a thick knobby root. 
⸙ It has no woody stem, and pale yellow-green flowers.
⸙ It's known for it's spicy, aromatic root. 
⸙ It's most preferred climate is warm and humid, and thrives in rich well-drained soil. 
⸙ The root has anti-nausea and anti-inflammatory properties, making it perfect to brew in teas and tinctures to boost the immune system and reduce fatigue.
⸙ Topically, it can be applied for joint pain and bruises.
