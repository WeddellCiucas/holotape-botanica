LILY
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a herbaceous flowering perennial plant, with upright stems 
⸙ It has large, trumpet-shaped flowers, often fragrant, and come in various colors like white, pink, orange, red, yellow and black
⸙ Prefers a moderately moist climate
⸙ Raw bulbs if eaten can cause nausea
⸙ Extracts can be used as poisons to deal high internal damage
⸙ Highly ornamental plants, strongly fragrant, old folk tales say that caution is advised not to sleep in a closed enclosure with a bouquet of lilies, to not risk suffocation.
⸙ Post-War Appalachia has an irradiated version they name the Radlily.
