────────────────────────────
LEEK
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a biennial plant related to onions and garlic.
⸙ It has long, cylindrical white stalks formed by tightly packed leaves. 
⸙ The leaves are flat, bluish green, and grow in fan-like shapes.
⸙ In its second year it grows a tall, flowering stalk topped with round purple or white flowers.
⸙ The white base and the leaves are edible.
⸙ It prefers cool, temperate climates and can tolerate light frost, but not extreme heat.
⸙ It's rich in nutrients and has anti-inflammatory and cardioprotective properties due to sulfur compounds, similar to garlic.
⸙ Traditionally used to treat respiratory issues and to boost immunity.
⸙ It's a key ingredient in many soups and broths, and has mild, onion-like flavoring.
