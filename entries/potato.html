────────────────────────────
POTATO
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Perennial plants, with green leafy stems. 
⸙ They grow white, pink or purple flowers with yellow middles. 
⸙ The edible parts are the tubers. 
⸙ The fruits are green.  
⸙ They are a cool-season plant that prefer loamy, well-drained soil.  
⸙ The tubers are high in nutrients, but can risk minor poisoning if eating raw.  
⸙ Every other part of the plant is poisonous.
