────────────────────────────
CANNABIS
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Also known as marijuana or hemp, it's an annual flowering herb
⸙ The leaves are palmate-like with 5-9 serrated leaflets  
⸙ It grows somewhere between 3-13 feet tall  
⸙ Produces flowers used for medicinal and psychoactive purposes;  
⸙ It thrives in warm to temperate climate  
⸙ The buds, or the dried flowers give out this 'mellowing' effect, as a psychoactive  
⸙ The leaves and stems have been used for centuries actually to craft fiber, rope and textiles.  
⸙ The oil from the seeds can be used in cooking or as a chemical solvent.
