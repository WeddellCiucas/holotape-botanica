GUAJILLO CHILI
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Perennial in warm climates, it produces glossy, deep red chilli peppers
⸙ The plant bears green leaves and white flowers
⸙ Prefers hot and arid climates
⸙ It's high in oxidants due to its capsaicin content, popular in dishes and used for flavoring
⸙ Mild pain dulling effects at low doses
