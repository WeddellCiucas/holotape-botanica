LIME
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's an evergreen shrub or tree, often thorny, with fragrant white blossoms and round green/yellowish fruits.
⸙ It typically grows in tropical or subtropical zones, but it's adaptable to acidic, neutral or alkaline soils. Does fairly well in sandy substrates.
⸙ It's a Vitamin C powerhouse, that supports immunity, collagen production and wound healing. 
⸙ Also has antimicrobial and antiseptic properties. 
⸙ Tasty in recipes and beverages especially.
