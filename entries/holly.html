────────────────────────────
HOLLY
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's an evergreen shrub, with glossy dark green leaves, with sharp and spiny edges  
⸙ The berries are bright and red, mostly decorative  
⸙ They grow densely, up to 10-25 feet tall;  
⸙ They prefer cool and temperate zones.  
⸙ The leaves contain mild caffeine-like compounds, so holly tea can be a good coffee substitute  
⸙ The berries are toxic, especially in large doses
