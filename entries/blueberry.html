────────────────────────────
BLUEBERRY
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Perennial flowering shrub that usually grows up between 1-3 ft. tall
⸙ It prefers cooler temperatures
⸙ It has adapted to nutrient-poor, acidic soil over time
⸙ The leaves are oval-shaped, smooth-edged, and the flowers are bell-shaped
⸙ Typically white or pale pink
⸙ The fruits are rich in antioxidants, fibers, vitamin C and K
⸙ Rich in natural sugars
⸙ Also contain high amounts of anthocyanins, or blue pigments that can be extracted
⸙ Seeds are safe to consume for fibers, the leaves however can only be consumed as tea in moderation.
⸙ The flowers provide little to no nutritional value
⸙ Everything else should be avoided.
