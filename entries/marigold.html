MARIGOLD
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a perennial herb that grows from 10 inches and up to 3 feet.
⸙ The flowers are bright orange or yellow, similar to daisies. 
⸙ The leaves are feathery, like ferns. 
⸙ The scent is pungent and the flowers are edible, they have a mildly bitter, peppery taste. 
⸙ They are pretty hardy, and thrive in dry climates. 
⸙ The flowers are used in natural pigments.
