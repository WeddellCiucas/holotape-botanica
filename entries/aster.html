ASTER
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Daisy-like plant that can grow between 1-4 feet tall, known to be late bloomers.
⸙ The flowers are purple/lavender in color or pink, with yellow centers.
⸙ They prefer meadows, roadsides and prairie edges. 
⸙ The roots are brewed to treat headaches, fevers and colds.
⸙ Flowers and leaves are edible, used fresh or dried in salads, teas and garnishes.
