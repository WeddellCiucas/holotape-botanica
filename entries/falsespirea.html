FALSE SPIREA
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a fast growing, multi-stemmed shrub, reaching up to 10 ft. under proper conditions. 
⸙ The leaves are reminiscent of mountain ash and produce large panicles of white flowers in early to mid-summer. 
⸙ It spreads aggressively via root suckers, forming dense colonies ideal for erosion control. 
⸙ It prefers full sun and moist soil, thriving in warm climates. 
⸙ This plant is often used for landscaping as ornamental and border/screen plants. 
⸙ The leaves and flowers contain hydrogen cyanide compounds, that can cause severe poisoning in excess.
