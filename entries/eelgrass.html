EELGRASS
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's an aquatic plant with long, flat, ribbon-like green leaves.
⸙ Commonly found in coastal bays, estuaries, and shallow marine waters. 
⸙ It provides habitat and oxygenation for aquatic life.
⸙ Contains polyphenols, making it a crafting base for detoxification items, and endurance for underwater exploration. Helps with pressure stabilization while submerged, and its extract can enhance lung capacity.
