SILT BEAN
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Silt beans are stalking plants that grow in the irradiated soil of the Appalachian wastelands especially. 
⸙ Its latin name is Fabaceae morte.
⸙ It's highly nutritive and can be used in a variety of local dishes.
⸙ Since it's part of the bean family, there's a good concentration of potassium in its compounds. 
⸙ Locals also use it to enhance the orange flavoring of the Orange Mentats.
