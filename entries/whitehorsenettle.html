WHITE HORSENETTLE PLANT
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Perennial herbaceous plant, with spiky thorns and coarse hair
⸙ Pale violet/white star-shaped flowers
⸙ Small, yellow/orange berries looking a lot like cherry tomatoes
⸙ Leaves are oval, lobed, and prickly underneath 
⸙ All parts of this plant are toxic when consumed raw
⸙ Thrives in arid and undisturbed soil, such as wastelands, roadsides and abandoned farmlands
⸙ The berries can be used in hallucinogenic chems and poisons
⸙ The leaf smoke can be used in paralytic darts
⸙ In very small doses, the root tincture can be used to build up poison immunity and resistance
