POBLANO PEPPER
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a bushy green plant with wide leaves  
⸙ Fresh peppers are dark green, heart shaped  
⸙ When ripe and dried, they turn a dark reddish-brown  
⸙ Thrives in warm, dry climates  
⸙ The pepper has a mild spiciness to it  
⸙ Besides its usual usage in cooking, capsaicin in small doses reduces joint pain  
⸙ Seeds may be harvested to create capsaicin-based weapons or chems.
