────────────────────────────
ROSE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Perennial flowering shrub 
⸙ Thorny, woody stems, and compound leaves with serrated edges
⸙ Large, fragrant flowers in a high variety of colors
⸙ The fruits called 'hips' are berries rich in nutrients
⸙ Thrive in temperate climates
⸙ The rose petals can be brewed in teas, or be turned into jam and used in pastries
⸙ The fruits are high in vitamin C and antioxidants 
⸙ Can be made into highly fragrant perfumes and aromatherapy that lowers stress
