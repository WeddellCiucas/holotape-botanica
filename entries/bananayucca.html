BANANA YUCCA
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a common plant found across the Mojave, near cliffs, deserts, and rocky outcrops.
⸙ It has sharp, sword-like leaves with pale yellow edges - distinguishable from other yucca plants. 
⸙ It has a cluster of thick, banana-shaped fruits at the center. 
⸙ It prefers arid desert climate, and sandy or volcanic soil. 
⸙ Historically has been used for food, fiber and medicine. 
⸙ It's rich in carbohydrates, vitamin C, and fibers, thus improving your immune system, digestion, and is ought to give you a quick energy boost.
⸙ Yucca sap is used to create a topical healing poultice or powder.
