────────────────────────────
FRAILEJON
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Tall, perennial shrub with thick stems topped with dense spirals of succulent leaves  
⸙ Older leaves remain attached to form insulation layers  
⸙ Prefers high-altitude ecosystems  
⸙ The flowers grow in daisy-like clusters and bloom peaks in July-August  
⸙ They are critical for capturing fog moisture  
⸙ Leaf tea soothes coughs and asthma  
⸙ Dried leaf fiber can be used as bedding or filler, also in insulation wraps
