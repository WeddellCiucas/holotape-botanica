WESTERN JUNIPER TREE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a coniferous evergreen tree that grows up to 10-50 feet tall. 
⸙ The bark is reddish-grey, fibrous and shedding, and the leaves are need-like. 
⸙ The berries are blue
⸙ Thrives in rocky, arid soils 
⸙ The juniper berries have been traditionally used in tonics and antiseptics. 
⸙ The needles can be distilled into a potent insect repellent.
