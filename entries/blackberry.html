BLACKBERRY
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a thorny, perennial shrub with biennial canes and edible fruits.
⸙ Blackberries are common in temperate zones, growing especially in undisturbed areas like woodland edges, streambanks, roadsides and overgrown ruins. 
⸙ The droplet black fruits are high in dietary fibers and vitamin C. 
⸙ The leaves are used in traditional tonics for digestive soothing and gut health support. 
⸙ They can also be applied as mouthwash for gums, throat and ulcers.
