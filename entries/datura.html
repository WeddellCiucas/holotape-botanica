────────────────────────────
DATURA
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Annual herb with spiny seed pods  
⸙ Flowers are large, trumpet-shaped, white to purple in color  
⸙ Also called the Devil's Trumpet and Thornapple  
⸙ Contains tropane alkaloids, such as atropine, scopolamine, and hyoscyamine  
⸙ All plant parts are highly toxic  
⸙ Ingestion can cause delirium, hallucinations, tachycardia, fever, photophobia, amnesia  
⸙ Fatal in high doses  
⸙ Potent neurotoxin.
