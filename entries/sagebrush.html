SAGEBRUSH
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a woody aromatic shrub in the same family as daisies
⸙ The leaves are silvery-grey, with a soft and fuzzy texture
⸙ It's usually 2-6 feet tall  
⸙ Has a herbal aroma.
⸙ It thrives in rocky, desert soil and harsh climates.
⸙ The tea leaves have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory purposes
⸙ Its strong scent can be mask scent against local fauna.
