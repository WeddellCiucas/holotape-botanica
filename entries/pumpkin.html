⸙ It's cultivated widely in temperate and warm zones. 
⸙ Grows in fields, gardens, and Vault farms.  
⸙ The fruit and seeds are rich in beta-carotene/Vitamin A, Vitamin C, vitamin E, fiber, potassium, magnesium, zinc, iron and other antioxidants.  
⸙ Boosts immunity and iron absorption which helps with disease resistance.  
⸙ Highly nutritive and grown as staple crop for morale, rations and nutrients.  
⸙ The seeds and pulp can be used in detox supplements and immunity kits.
