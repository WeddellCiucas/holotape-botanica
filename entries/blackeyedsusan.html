────────────────────────────
BLACK-EYED SUSAN
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Perennial wildflower, with bright yellow petals surrounding a dark brown/purplish central disk  
⸙ It can grow up to 3 feet tall, with hair stems and leaves  
⸙ Usually blooms in summer and early fall  
⸙ Incredibly sturdy and resistant to harsh climates  
⸙ Dried flowers brewed in tea boost immunity  
⸙ Root tincture gives poison resistance  
⸙ Has mild antibiotic properties
