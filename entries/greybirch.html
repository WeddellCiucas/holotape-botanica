GREY BIRCH
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Small, fast growing trees that can reach 20-40 feet. 
⸙ The bark is silvery grey with black markings. 
⸙ The leaves are triangular, fine-toothed. 
⸙ They prefer cold-to-temperate climates, and grow in poor or sandy soil. 
⸙ The leaves have mild diuretic effects, used in herbal teas for detoxification. 
⸙ Turning the bark into charcoal can be used in an anti-toxin filter or water purifying cartridge. 
⸙ Caution is advised when choosing your tree to harvest. The bark can be infected with fungi such as Chaga or Polypores. Consuming or using the bark can cause hallucinations.
