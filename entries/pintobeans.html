PINTO BEANS
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Climbing vine with green leaves, that produces pink and purple pea-like blossoms. 
⸙ Each pod contains several beige and brown beans. 
⸙ It prefers the warm, and frost-free climate of the Mojave. 
⸙ Rich in protein and provides slow-release energy, perfect for long journeys. 
⸙ Supports digestion, improves blood health and counters anemia, commonly found in scavengers with a poor diet, as a result of its high iron concentration. 
⸙ Low in fat and high in B vitamins.
