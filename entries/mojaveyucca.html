MOJAVE YUCCA
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Similar to the Banana Yucca, the Joushua Tree and the Mojave Agave.
⸙ It's a perennial flowering shrub, with a dense rosette of long, rigid, spiky leaves.
⸙ Base is woody and has a tall flower stalk, with white to purplish blooms.
⸙ The fruits are fleshy, short, banana-shaped.
⸙ Prefers arid desert climates and rocky slopes.
⸙ Can survive extreme heat.
⸙ The fruits provide minor hydration and are highly nutritious.
⸙ The root can be ground into soap.
⸙ The leaves are used in rope making or tribal weaving.
⸙ The nectar can be fermented into a type of mead.
