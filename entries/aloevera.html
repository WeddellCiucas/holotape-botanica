ALOE VERA
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a succulent with thick, fleshy green leaves; 
⸙ They often have small white teeth or spines all along so caution is advised, 
⸙ The interior is filled with a gel-like substance
⸙ The blossoms are yellow-orange
⸙ It's drought-resistant, perfect for the arid climate of the Mojave
⸙ The gel from the leaves is a natural burn and wound treatment 
⸙ The latex/sap of the aloe however is fairly bitter, and acts as a mild laxative. \
⸙ The gel is refreshing and provides hydration.
