TATO
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ The tato is a hybrid plant between a tomato and a potato, grown after the Great War.
⸙ The new fruit looks like a tomato, but it's brown on the inside.
⸙ It's commonly cultivated all across the wasteland, known for its nutritive properties.
⸙ Some describe its taste to be "disgusting".
⸙ It's a staple food of the Wasteland used in various dishes.
