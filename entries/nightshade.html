NIGHTSHADE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Also known as the Belladonna, it's a perennial plant.
⸙ It has dark green, ovate leaves with purple veins.
⸙ Bell-shaped purple flowers with greenish tips.
⸙ Glossy black berries, highly toxic.
⸙ Grows around 2-5 feet tall, woody stem, often seen in shade and woodland edges.
⸙ Grows in cool and shady climates.
⸙ The berries are highly toxic because of these compounds:
⸙ 1. Atropine that causes paraysis, hallucination, dry mouth and rapid heartbeat.
⸙ 2. Scopolamine: that causes amnesia, sedation and psychosis-like effects.
