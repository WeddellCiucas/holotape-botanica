────────────────────────────
VALERIAN
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Perennial herb that grows up to 3-5 feet tall  
⸙ The stems are hollowed and the leaves are feathery  
⸙ The flowers are pale pink or white, with a sweet scent  
⸙ The roots are thick, gnarled and highly aromatic  
⸙ They prefer cool, moist areas  
⸙ The root extract has calming effects  
⸙ Aids with sleep problems, anxiety, and other similar mental states
⸙ Can also be brewed into teas.  
⸙ Has an effect similar to catnip when given to cats.
