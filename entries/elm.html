ELM
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Deciduous tree with large canopy
⸙ Leaves are oval, serrated, often asymmetrical, hairy beneath
⸙ Flowers and fruit grow in wind-pollinated clusters
⸙ Prefer temperate climates, forests, and riparian zones
⸙ The inner bark can be brewed into a tea or poultice to soothe burns and throat infections
⸙ The fiber can be used for rope, splints, and bedding
⸙ It's rich in mucilage, but can slow absorption of other chems and medicine if taken too close together
