────────────────────────────
TOMATO
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Annually grown plants with trailing vines  
⸙ Small, red, orange, or yellow fruits  
⸙ Soft green stems and bright yellow flowers  
⸙ The taste is sweet and tangy  
⸙ Prefers warm and sunny regions  
⸙ The fruits are rich in vitamin C and lycopene  
⸙ Highly appreciated in various dishes.
⸙ Smaller tomato species are referred to as 'Cherry Tomatoes'
