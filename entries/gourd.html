GOURD
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's an annual vine or bush, the leaves are broad and green, and has long trailing vines 
⸙ The fruit is edible, large, green, orange or striped in color 
⸙ Has yellow star-shaped flowers prior fruiting 
⸙ Prefers temperate and arid climates 
⸙ The fruit is rich in carbs
⸙ The seeds are a source of healthy fats and magnesium 
⸙ Can obtain vegetable starch for various purposes, including crafting adhesives
