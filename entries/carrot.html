────────────────────────────
CARROT
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Biennial root vegetable  
⸙ The root is orange and conical  
⸙ The leaves are feathery and the flowers are small, white, umbrella-like  
⸙ Prefers cool to temperate climates in loose and sandy soils  
⸙ Rich in beta-carotene or Vitamin A  
⸙ Improves vision and boost immune system.
