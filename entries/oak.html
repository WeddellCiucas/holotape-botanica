────────────────────────────
OAK TREE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Tree that can reach up to 60-100 feet height, with broad, spreading canopies
⸙ Thick, ridged bark that's grey to dark brown
⸙ Lobed leaves
⸙ Produces acorns as seeds
⸙ Has a long lifespan and prefers temperate climates
⸙ The acorns are edible if leached, and can be used in wasteland flour, nut pastes, or a survival stew as they are high in carbs and fat
⸙ Powdered bark can be used in anti-inflammatory salves
⸙ Leaves can be used in herbal teas
⸙ Wood is incredibly versatile 
⸙ The tannin-rich resin can be used for waterproofing
