ONION
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's annual bulbous plant with hollow bluish-green leaves.
⸙ The underground swelling bulb composed of layered, modified leaves is the edible part of the plant most commonly.
⸙ It thrives in temperate zones.
⸙ It's rich in quercetin, sulfur compounds, vitamins, fibers and potassium.
⸙ Supports heart health, immune function, blood sugar regulation and digestive wellness.
⸙ The cooked bulb is highly nutritious.
⸙ The tea can help boost the immune system. 
⸙ Can however produce lachrymatory compounds that irritate eyes and the respiratory airways.
