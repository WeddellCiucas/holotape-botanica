DOGWOOD
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a genus of woody trees and shrubs, with showy cluster of small flowers and fruits. 
⸙ They are native to temperate areas, moist to dry forests, along wetlands and forest edges. 
⸙ The root and the inner bark infusion can be used as fever reducer and pain reliever. 
⸙ The wood is dense hard, and fine-grained, preferred for tool handles and speciality woodworking. 
⸙ The inner bark twigs were used as natural toothbrushes and gum hardeners. 
⸙ The bark can also be used to craft dye and ink.
