────────────────────────────
TRICANTHOS
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Also known as honey locust, it's a tree with feathery leaves, long curved seed pods that contain sweet pulp and small, hard seeds.  
⸙ They thrive in arid climates, but also in temperate zones like the Commonwealth or the Appalachia  
⸙ The pulp is a natural sweetener  
⸙ The hardwood is perfect for crafting and carpentry  
⸙ The seeds are edible if roasted
