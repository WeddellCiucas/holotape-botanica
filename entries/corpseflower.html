────────────────────────────
CORPSE FLOWER
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ According to Old World entries, it used to be one of the largest flowers in the world, measuring up to 10 feet in height, before radiation has mutated the local flora.  
⸙ It features a giant central spadix with a ruffled maroon spathe  
⸙ It blooms extremely rarely, even 5-10 years, lasting only about 24-48 hours. 
⸙ I had the honor of witnessing one in mid-bloom.  
⸙ It's famous for its foul stench, resembling rotting flesh  
⸙ Prefers humid, tropical climates  
⸙ The bloom core when heated can be used to create a 'corpse pheromone' that can attract feral creatures  
⸙ If prepared a certain way, the compound in its flower can cause hallucinations
