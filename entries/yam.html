YAM
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Yams are climbing or trailing herbaceous perennials that produce starchy underground tubers. Domestically-cultivated yams are widely planted, but wild-growing yams need further processing before cultivation.
⸙ Wild yams have natural toxins.
⸙ They are dense in carbohydrates, fiber and potassium, and have beneficial antioxidants.
⸙ Their fiber improve insulin sensitivity and slow glucose release.
⸙ They are also a reliable stable for calorie-dense endurance sustenance.
