PATCHOULI
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's technically a tropical plant, or at the very least grows in warmer climates
⸙ Highly sensitive to cold temperature
⸙ It can grow between 2-3 feet tall
⸙ The stem is soft, similar to mint, and the leaves are large soft and fuzzy
⸙ Highly aromatic
⸙ The flowers are small, pale, pink-white
⸙ Patchouli is an aromatic herb, but is typically used externally or for aromatherapy purposes
⸙ Internal consumption is technically not advised, only in small doses as a weak herbal tea
⸙ Supposedly has a mild calming effect
⸙ It's very bitter, however, and can cause nausea if it's too concentrated
⸙ Externally, it has anti-inflammatory and skin-regenerating properties.
⸙ Naturally repels insects, including moths
