HONEY MESQUITE TREE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a thorny shrub or a small tree with a deep taproot, paired spines, bipinnate leaves and long seed pods.
⸙ It's native to the pre-War Southwestern US. 
⸙ Very drought tolerant with succulent wood and branches.
⸙ The flowers bloom from March to November as observed.
⸙ The seeds and pods can be roasted and ground into a high-protein flour, rich in calcium, iron and fiber.
⸙ Leaf tea and sap can be used to treat fevers and coughs.
⸙ Gum and bark tinctures can be used as antiseptics and topical infection remedies.
⸙ Wood is dense and aromatic with various uses.
⸙ Can fix nitrogen into the soil, good for agriculture.
