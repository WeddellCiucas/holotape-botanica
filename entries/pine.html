────────────────────────────
PINE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's an evergreen coniferous tree.  
⸙ Leaves are needle-like that grow in bundles.  
⸙ Prefers more temperate zones.  
⸙ The seeds are edible, rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants.  
⸙ Recommended to use the needles and fruit in teas and syrups to treat cold symptoms and respiratory issues.  
⸙ Pine bark extract for anti-inflammatory and cardiovascular benefits.  
⸙ Pine resin and oil for poultices, to use for antiseptic, antibacterial properties. 
⸙ Used topical for skin infections, arthritis and wound sealing.
