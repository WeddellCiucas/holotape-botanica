────────────────────────────
JOSHUA TREE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Evergreen tree-like succulent
⸙ Thick trunk, with spiny dagger-shaped leaves
⸙ Branches twist in irregular shapes
⸙ Produces clusters of greenish-white flowers that bloom briefly in spring
⸙ The bark is fibrous and together with the leaf strands can be used in fire-starters
⸙ The leaves can also be used to make cords, nets, or arm wraps for slings
⸙ The flowers can be brewed into a mild tea
⸙ The roasted seeds can be used in trail snacks
