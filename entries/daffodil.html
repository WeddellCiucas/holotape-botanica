DAFFODIL
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Perennial bulbous plant  
⸙ Grows up from 6 to 20 inches  
⸙ It has a white or yellow trumpet-shaped flower  
⸙ The central flower is surrounded by six more petals  
⸙ The leaves are long, flat, grass-like  
⸙ Has a mild, pleasant scent  
⸙ Grows in temperate zones  
⸙ As pretty as it is, it's highly toxic!  
⸙ The bulb is a potent toxin as it contains lycorine and galantamine  
⸙ The flower oil can cause mild hallucination or nausea
