────────────────────────────
CRANBERRY
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Low growing, have tiny evergreen leaves and reddish stems  
⸙ Produce small pink flowers, and firm red berries  
⸙ Found typically in wetlands, marshes and bogs  
⸙ They prefer cool to temperate climates  
⸙ The raw berries are high in vitamin C and antioxidants  
⸙ Can help heal urinary and digestive tract illnesses
