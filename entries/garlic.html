────────────────────────────
GARLIC
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Perennial plant with narrow, flat green leaves. 
⸙ It grows from a bulb made of cloves beneath the soil;
⸙ It's white/purple, and has a strong scent.
⸙ The flowers are white/pink in mature plants. 
⸙ Prefers cool to warm temperate climate
⸙ The cloves have antimicrobial and antioxidant properties; 
⸙ Crushed garlic can be used in poultices to fight infection 
⸙ Immunity booster; 
⸙ Recommended as flavoring in cooking!
