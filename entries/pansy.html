────────────────────────────
PANSY
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Small and delicate flowers with rounded petals shaped like distinctive 'faces' due to their multicolor blotches;  
⸙ They can be purple, yellow, blue, blue, white, burgundy or black.  
⸙ They grow to be 6-9 inches tall and have a mild scent, and edible petals.  
⸙ They prefer cool and shaded areas.  
⸙ The extract or tea can be used as a mild sedative.  
⸙ The flowers can be used in dyes and warpaint.
