────────────────────────────
HEMLOCK
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a biennial herb that can grow up to 5-10 feet tall  
⸙ Has smooth, purple-spotted stem and fern-like lacy leaves  
⸙ Small white umbrella-shaped flowers  
⸙ Carrot-like smell  
⸙ I can not stress this enough, it's highly toxic!  
⸙ All parts of the flower contain coniine, a potent neurotoxin  
⸙ The sap causes paralysis and suffocation as it blocks neuromuscular junctions  
⸙ The root is an alkaloid-rich storage organ.  
⸙ The flowers can easily be mistaken for the wild carrot, which is a safe plant; making hemlock even more dangerous.
