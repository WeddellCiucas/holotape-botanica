────────────────────────────
COYOTE TOBACCO
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Annual herb with sticky hairs growing on stem  
⸙ Prefers dry, sandy soils, along stream banks, railroad edges and post-fire terrain  
⸙ The flowers are narrow and tubular, white to greenish-white, shaped like trumpets  
⸙ The leaves are lanceolate, glandular and sticky  
⸙ They have a high concentrate of nicotine, even 2-3x higher than in common tobacco.
