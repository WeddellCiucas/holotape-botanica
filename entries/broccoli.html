────────────────────────────
BROCCOLI
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a cruciferous vegetable in the cabbage family, featuring tightly packed green florets on thick stalks surrounded by leaves.
⸙ It thrives in cool and moist climates, with full sun and mild shade.
⸙ Prefers well-drained, fertile soil.
⸙ It's loaded with fibers, vitamins and antioxidants.
⸙ It supports immune health, bone health and heart function. 
⸙ There are many ways to cook it.
