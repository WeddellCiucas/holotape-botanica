────────────────────────────
JALAPEÑO PEPPER
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a cultivar of the Capsicum annuum, with a medium heat rating of 4.000-8.500 Scoville Heat Units. 
⸙ It ripens from green to red or yellow, becoming slightly sweeter and hotter when mature.
⸙ It thrives in warm climates, sensitive to cold and frost.
⸙ It's rich in vitamin C, and being a source of capsaicin, it has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. It supports heart health and reduces cell damage.
⸙ Capsaicin blocks pain receptors, so it can help with pain relief via topical application.
⸙ Also improves glucose control and increases metabolism.
