────────────────────────────
RICE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Annual grass with narrow, pointed green leaves
⸙ Long, nodding heads bearing rows of small grain husks
⸙ Grows best in standing water or seasonally flooding fields
⸙ Thrives in warm, wet areas, bayous and river deltas
⸙ It's a source of B vitamins
⸙ Soothes burns and rashes 
⸙ The grains can be cooked, or can be cooked into dried rice cakes for long shelf life
⸙ The rice can be fermented and brewed into wine
⸙ The rice can also be turned into rice flour.
