FEVER BLOSSOM
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a brambly plant with climbing brown branches, leaves, and bright blue flowers.
⸙ The bright blue flowers are edible, but they can provide mild radiation poisoning.
⸙ However they can be purified and steeped into teas for slight regenerative properties.
