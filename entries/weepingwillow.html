WEEPING WILLOW
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ They grow to be about 30-50 feet tall; 
⸙ They have long, drooping branches and narrow, lance-shaped green leaves
⸙ They create a cascading, umbrella like canopy
⸙ The bark is pale gey/brown
⸙ They are often found alongside river banks, streams and rivers, as such they thrive in moist soil and temperate climates
⸙ The bark naturally contains salicin, a compound dubbed as a natural aspirin, so an extract can be used as a mild painkiller
⸙ Branches are naturally used for basketweaving.
