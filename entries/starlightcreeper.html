STARLIGHT BERRY
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a plant growing throughout Appalachia's forested region. 
⸙ The bush brambles and leaves are sharp, pink, and the berries are yellow.
⸙ They're yellow and edible for humans. 
⸙ The locals use the berries as flavoring in soups, steeped in teas, or in sweet pastries.
