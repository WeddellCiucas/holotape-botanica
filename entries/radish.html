────────────────────────────
RADISH
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a fast growing root vegetable related to the mustard family, with edible roots and leaves.
⸙ There exists numerous variants that are often planted in the hydroponics bay of functioning Vaults.
⸙ It prefers loose, fertile soil, and grows best in cool temperatures. 
⸙ Some varieties are winter-acclimated, but that can be easily replicated in artificial environments. 
⸙ They are a pretty low-calorie food source, fairly hydrating and packed with fiber and vitamins.
⸙ They can be used in various dishes, but also dried and easily stored.
⸙ The radish also contains an anti-fungal agent such as RsAFP2, so  might be used in anti-fungal treatments and antidotes.
