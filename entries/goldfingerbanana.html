GOLD FINGER BANANA TREE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's actually a banana hybrid tree, bred to only grow up to 6-10 feet tall. 
⸙ Grows in clumps, with large broad leaves. 
⸙ The fruits are sweet, golden-yellow. 
⸙ They prefer warm, tropical climates. They seem to thrive in Nevada. 
⸙ The fruits are high in potassium, helps with digestion, and the slower glucose release in the bloodstream make them perfect to include in survival rations.
