RHODODENDRON
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a low-lying, desert-adapted shrub with narrow feathers and red/pink blossoms  
⸙ It looks much duller than its original counterpart, but also far more resilient, thriving in dry and arid climates  
⸙ The flower and leaves are mildly toxic neurotoxin. 
⸙ Can cause nausea, hallucinations or paralysis in large doses.  
⸙ They are pretty enough to be considered ornamental plants and shrubbery, for your wasteland garden.
