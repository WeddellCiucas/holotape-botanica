SASSAFRAS TREE
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Medium sized tree, with oval, mitten-shaped and three-lobed leaves
⸙ Bark is deeply furrowed, aromatic when scrapped 
⸙ Produces small yellow flowers in spring, and blue/black berries on red stalks after fruiting
⸙ The roots and bark emit a strong, spicy-sweet scent
⸙ Prefers moist, temperate climates
⸙ The bark and root can be boiled and brewed into teas
⸙ The oil extract can be made into perfumes, or further refined into psychoactive chems due to its safrole content
⸙ The wood smoke has a calming incense
⸙ The berry pulp is a mild stimulant
⸙ Powdered leaves can be used to thicken stews
