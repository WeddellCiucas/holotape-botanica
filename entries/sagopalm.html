SAGO PALM
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Short, stout, woody trunk, and feather-like dark green leaves
⸙ They reach from 2-3 feet up to 10 feet in perfect conditions
⸙ They produce large cones, not flowers, and female plants may bear toxic orange seeds
⸙ They prefer hot and arid climate and soil
⸙ The seeds are toxic if consumed raw
⸙ Used for starch when cooked
⸙ The resin can be used for sticky trap and material purposes
