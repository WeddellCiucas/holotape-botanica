LIMA BEAN
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a perennial bean plant that grows in a bushy, climbing vine with trifoliate leaves
⸙ Produces flat, crescent-shaped pods that contain 2-4 large seeds
⸙ Beans are white, green or mottled purple
⸙ The flowers are small, white to pale violet
⸙ Thrives in warm, semi-arid climates
⸙ Highly nutritious in dishes, cooked in stews and other recipes
⸙ Dried beans can be used as trail mixes for their long shelf life
⸙ Rich in iron and fibers
⸙ High protein and starch 
⸙ Raw beans can be toxic
