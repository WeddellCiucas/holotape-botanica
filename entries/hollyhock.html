────────────────────────────
HOLLYHOCK
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Tall, biennial flowery plant from the mallow family, that can reach up to 4-8 feet tall.  
⸙ The flowers are large, and can be pink, red, purple, yellow or white.  
⸙ The leaves are broad and fuzzy.  
⸙ They grow in temperate or arid zones with moderate irrigation.  
⸙ The flowers can be used in soothing teas.  
⸙ The leaves are mucilaginous, and can soothe skin wounds and burns.  
⸙ The petals are pigmented enough to use as natural dye for fabrics or as warpaint.
