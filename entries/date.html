────────────────────────────
DATE PALM
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a long-living, slow-growing palm, thriving in arid and semi-arid climates. 
⸙ It's also historically known as one of the oldest cultivated fruit tree. 
⸙ The dates are a rich nutritional source.
⸙ High in natural sugars, potassium and vitamins.
⸙ Energy-dense calories, they're ideal for trail mixes and survival rations.
