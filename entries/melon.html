────────────────────────────
MELON
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ An annual trailing vine plant, it produces sweet, fleshy fruits with yellow flowers. The rinds and fruit colors differ by type.
⸙ Prefers warm regions with rich and loamy soil.
⸙ The fruit is rich in water and vitamins.
⸙ The dried fruity slices are lightweight and preserve energy well.
⸙ The seeds are rich in fatty acids and proteins.
