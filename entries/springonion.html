SPRING ONION
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Typically perennial or biennial herbs.
⸙ They have long, hollow green stalks and small white bulbs at the base.
⸙ Grow in tight clumps.
⸙ Mild onion aroma, and looks like grass from afar.
⸙ They thrive in cool to warm climates.
⸙ They prefer fertile, well-drained soils.
⸙ Typically used for cooking, high in vitamin C and K, with digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
⸙ The ash of the residue can be used as great natural fertilizer for its high potassium content.
