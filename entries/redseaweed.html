RED SEAWEED
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a type of bright red to deep crimson seaweed with branching or feathery structures.  
⸙ It's typically soft and gelatinous to touch.  
⸙ It's edible, and has components such as agar and carrageenan often used in food gelling or cosmetics.  
⸙ Lives in shallow marine waters, tidal pools, and reef zones.  
⸙ Its gelling properties can be used in gel-bases or water-retention compounds.  
⸙ High in iodine, supporting thyroid health.  
⸙ Antiviral activity against enveloped viruses, to study for cures.
