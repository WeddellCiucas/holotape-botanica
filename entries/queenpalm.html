────────────────────────────
QUEEN PALM
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a tree in the palm family that can grown about 30-50 feet/ 
⸙ The trunk is smooth and grey
⸙ The crown is composed of feathery, arching fronds
⸙ They can reach 15 feet and are fanlike
⸙ It's often used as a decorative landscaping palm, especially around the New Vegas Strip
⸙ It produces yellow/orange fruits similar to dates. 
⸙ Prefers hot, semi-arid to tropical conditions
⸙ Highly nutritious, contains sugars and moistures; recommended to dry and keep as trail mix. 
⸙ Also high in potassium and Vitamin A.
