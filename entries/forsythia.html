FORSYTHIA
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's a shrub bearing bright yellow, four-petaled bell shaped flowers in early spring, even before the leaves can even emerge. 
⸙ The height can range between 2-10 ft. depending on the subspecies. 
⸙ It's fast growing, drought-tolerable and thrives in full sun and warm climates. 
⸙ It was traditionally used to treat fevers, sore throats and respiratory infections.
