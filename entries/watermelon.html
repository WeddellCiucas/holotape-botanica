WATERMELON
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ It's an annual vine crop from the gourd family
⸙ It has a long, sprawling vine with coarse leaves
⸙ The fruits are large, round to oblong
⸙ The flesh is pink-red and produces small yellow flowers before fruiting
⸙ The seeds are black, brown or white
⸙ Prefers hot and sunny climates and sandy soil
⸙ The fruit has a high water content and provides wonderful hydration, be careful with the mild diuretic effects though
⸙ Roasted seeds are rich in fat and proteins so they make amazing energy snacks on the go
⸙ The rinds can be cooked, boiled and pickled, even used in brahmin feed
⸙ The juice is amazing and refreshing, it's better than cola.
