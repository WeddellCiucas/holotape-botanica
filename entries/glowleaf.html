GLOW LEAF
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Or Experiment NRT-001 is an experimental plant found and researched by a field scribe. 
⸙ Being found at the mouth of a river, it's suggested it thrives in humid and moist soil. 
⸙ The roots are thick and the leaves are large, bluish-green. 
⸙ It's normally edible, can be brewed into teas and has regenerative properties, but it's highly addictive.
