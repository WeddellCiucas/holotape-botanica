COLOCASIA
──────────────────────────── 


⸙ Colloquially known as Elephant Ear, it's a mainly decorative plant.  
⸙ Grows between 3-6 feet tall.  
⸙ Distinguished by its gigantic heart-shaped leaves  
⸙ Grows in clumps.  
⸙ It prefers hot, humid and moist climate and soil.  
⸙ Plant is toxic if raw because of it's high calcium oxalate crystal concentration. 
⸙ It can cause hallucinations and sickness.  
⸙ The tuber however is a high calorie food source.  
⸙ The young leaves can be boiled into soups.  
⸙ The hardened mature leaves can be used as plates, water collectors or for improvised shelter.
